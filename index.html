About

Greenwalls by Oxyzone Biogreen Technologies is an industry-leading commercial installer and maintainer of living and preserved green wall designs, with presence in major markets across the Hyderabad. We provide consultation, design, installation and service for high-impact, biophilic-minded green walls. Our expertise in interior living walls and preserved moss walls is unmatched in the green wall space. We are committed to providing peace of mind to commercial property managers and office managers through responsiveness and quality.

What to expect

From the moment you contact us, refining and delivering your vision will be our priority. Expect responsive communication, true consultancy, and transparency throughout the design phase to help develop your project from an idea to a finished product.
